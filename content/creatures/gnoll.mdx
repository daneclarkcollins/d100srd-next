Gnolls are hominoid being found in many climates of Atheles. They have many of the same characteristics of hyenas; large, hunched shoulders, yellow to brown fur, and a strong muzzle filled with sharp teeth.

They live in large tribes, in a hunter-gatherer culture. They are often at odds with any other intelligent being who occupy the same territories.

*—Zoological Encyclopedia*

Gnolls can have many Skills and Characteristics. They can advance as any other intelligent creature in Atheles. Gnoll culture is fascinated by fire, and they often feature these motifs on their armor and weapons. Shamans who can cast fire magic or create alchemist fire are highly revered.
