Ettercaps are vile things... maybe even the vilest. They seem to be a mix of the worst aspects of spiders and demons. They are cowardly creatures who delight in attacking the weak and feeding on anything they can catch, preferably without a fight.

I was once a victim of an ettercap. I had come unprepared to its lair, believing that a strong arm and a blade were all that I needed to clear through the webs of my prey. At first, it seemed it was, until I was deep in its hollowed-out forest lair. Soon, the webs became thick, and my blade became stuck. I found myself bound and trapped. And then it came to feed on me. The ettercap tortured me for days, drinking my blood and taunting me with its horrible, guttural voice, prodding me awake with its bite or sting if I fell asleep. What little sleep I did get was filled with most awful dreams. I truly believed that I would die there.

And if not for Delgon, I would have been another dessicated husk in the ettercap's webs.

— Etahn

When I saw Etahn enter the ettercap's lair, I was too far away for him to hear me call to him. I tried to warn him. I knew his blade would not clear the web, and I had no means to help. If I followed him in, we would both die.

But I knew that the ettercap always takes time with its prey, knowing that its victim is going nowhere. So I ran to the nearest town where I had rented a room. I had been studying the ettercap for some time, and believed I was on the verge of discovering a solvent that would dissolve the ettercap's web. While I worked, I prayed that I would not be too late.

The solvent worked. The web dissolved easily upon contact, melting into a sickening slime that I was forced to trudge through until I reached Etahn. As I freed him, I felt a bite on my back. Now, it was Etahn who saved me, pouncing upon the creature, ramming his sword through its skull, and despite his weakened state, somehow carrying me back to town, whereupon he collapsed as more help arrived.

— Delgon

**Web**
As a move or standard action, ettercap can shoot a web up to 8 times per day, with a range of 50'. On a success, the target becomes Entangled. Webs can restrain creatures with up to double the ettercap's SIZ.

An entangled creature may escape with a DEX check or burst free with a STR check, both *Difficult*.

Ettercaps can also create sheet webs 5–60 ft across to trap prey. Creatures must succeed at a Spot check to notice the web before entering it. Failure results in becoming Entangled.

Web sections can be destroyed, and fire burns away one 5 ft section per round.

Ettercaps move freely on their own webs and can instantly detect the location of any creature touching them.
