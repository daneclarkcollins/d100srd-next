Miglins are small fey from the Navirim. They roam the dark plains of the other side, attacking lone creatures and feasting on them. They do much the same in Uteria. Few creatures are capable of crossing over in physical form without great effort or magical assistance, but miglins do so more easily than others.

They often hide in the forests and plains waiting for easy prey.

*—Oharad, Cryptonombus of the Navirim*
