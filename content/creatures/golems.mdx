Golems are magical creatures created through a ritual and programmed to perform a specific task. This task was etched into its body during its creation ritual. Golems have no thoughts or mind of their own; it only performs the task it was created for.

A golem can be reprogrammed by erasing the etched instructions and adding new ones, but this may not be successful. Please see the Golem Reprogramming chart below. Most golems will resist and attack if their instructions are being rewritten.

**Golem Special Traits:**

- Immune to critical hits, mind magic, disease, death effects, paralysis, poison, sleep, stun, or any effect that requires a Stamina check.
- Damage spells do 1/2 damage unless otherwise noted
- Dark Vision 60'
- Does not Dodge or Parry, unless its instructions command it to

### Golem Reprogramming Chart

| d100 | Reprogramming effect |
| --- | --- |
| 1-30 | Golem becomes reprogrammed |
| 31-60 | Golem wildly attacks those around it until destroyed |
| 61-100 | Golem loses its enchantment and becomes innert |
