# 11.1 SagaBorn D100 Bestiary

The **SagaBorn d100 Creature Compendium** is a complete bestiary designed for the _SagaBorn d100 RPG_, a system built on the foundation of **[Basic Roleplaying (BRP)](https://www.chaosium.com/basic-roleplaying)**. Inspired by the classic _Monster Manuals_ of tabletop roleplaying, this book collects creatures both familiar and unique, giving [StoryGuide's](http://localhost:8200/sagaborn-d100-srd-home/10-storyguides-codex/) and players a powerful resource for populating their campaigns with threats, allies, and mysteries.

The creatures presented here are drawn from the **[Dark Return setting](https://www.darkreturn.com)**, SagaBorn’s dark fantasy world of Uteria, where shadows linger and every monster has a story. While rooted in this setting, the monsters are designed with flexibility in mind: they can be used in **any BRP or D100 campaign**, from gritty survival horror to high fantasy adventures. This balance of lore-rich design and universal mechanics makes the Creature Compendium both a world guide and a practical monster manual.

**More:**

Unlike many bestiaries that focus only on monsters, this compendium provides full **d100 stat blocks** tailored to the SagaBorn ruleset, with clear formatting for quick reference during play. From common animals and beasts to terrifying horrors of the Dark Return setting, each entry includes lore, stats, and optional harvesting notes for players who want deeper interaction with the world.

For those familiar with **Chaosium’s BRP system** and other percentile-based games, the **SagaBorn d100 Bestiary** feels immediately accessible. Everything is percentile-driven, ensuring that rolls, attacks, defenses, and skills use the intuitive d100 format. Whether you are coming from BRP, RuneQuest, Call of Cthulhu, or any other d100 system, this compendium serves as both a monster manual and a worldbuilding guide for SagaBorn.

With dozens of original monsters, balanced stat tiers (from minions to bosses), and easy-to-use challenge values, the Creature Compendium is the perfect tool for any group seeking a **BRP bestiary for d100 play**. It is at once a familiar monster manual and a unique expansion into the dark fantasy world of Uteria, where every creature tells a story.
