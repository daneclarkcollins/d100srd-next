Reavers are towering Ashiron fey that delight in terror and slaughter. Their lean, blackened bodies seem carved from living shadow, while ember-red eyes burn beneath horned brows. They serve as hunters and executioners for the Ashiron courts, descending upon their prey with claw and spell before feasting upon fear itself.

Unlike many fey, reavers revel in direct violence. They are relentless pursuers, regenerating from nearly any wound unless struck by cold iron, the ancient bane of their kind.

—Etahn, *Creature Compendium*

**Regeneration 5**

At the beginning of each of the reaver's turns, it regains 5 HP provided it has at least 1 HP remaining.

Damage inflicted by Cold Iron cannot be regenerated. A reaver slain by Cold Iron cannot regenerate.

**Spell-Like Abilities**

A reaver may cast the following spells without spending Mana:

**At Will:** Mage Fire

**3/day:** Fear

Spellcasting uses the reaver's Spellcraft skill.

**Spellcasting**

Mana: 30

Reavers are natural spellcasters and commonly know offensive fire and fear magic. StoryGuides should customize their spell selection to suit the encounter.

**Fear Aura**

The oppressive presence of a reaver unnerves nearby creatures.

Any enemy that begins its turn within 20 feet of the reaver must immediately make a Horror check (2/1d6). A creature that succeeds is immune to that reaver's Fear Aura for the next 24 hours.

**Fly**

A reaver possesses powerful supernatural wings, allowing it to fly at its full movement rate with exceptional agility.

**Darkvision**

A reaver can see normally in darkness out to 60 feet, provided there is at least the faintest available light.

**Cold Iron**

Weapons made of Cold Iron bypass the reaver's Regeneration and inflict an additional +2 damage after all other modifiers are applied.
