Ettins are giant humanoid creatures who live in the hills and mountains of Atheles. They average 13' in height and weight nearly 5,000 lbs. The most unique feature ettins possess is that they have two heads, each of which each controls their own side of the body—and not always in cooperation with the other. In fact, they are not very intelligent and spend a great deal of time arguing with one another. Whenever they are capable of working together, they rely on raiding, stealing, and violence to survive. Whenever this happens, they are a force to be reckoned with.

*—Creaturi Obscuria*

**Special Abilities**

**Superior Two-Weapon Fighting**
The ettin wields a weapon in each hand without taking penalties for two-weapon attacks. Each head controls one arm independently, allowing full attack routines with no off-hand penalties.

**Powerful Attack**
Before making melee attack rolls in a round, the ettin can suffer a -10 melee attack roll penalty while gaining +1d8 to the melee damage roll.
