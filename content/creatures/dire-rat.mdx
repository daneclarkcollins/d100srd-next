Dire rats are omnivorous scavengers, but will attack in defense of their nests and territories.

A dire rat can grow to be up to 4 feet long and weigh over 50 pounds. They are monstrously large versions of a normal rat, their bodies covered in long fur and protruding bone plates.

*—Zoological Encyclopedia*
