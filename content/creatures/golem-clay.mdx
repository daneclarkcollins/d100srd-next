A massive monstrosity of fired earth, clay golems are fearsome foes. They can be carved in all manner of shapes, but a large humanoid is most common. Clay golems are prone to have bouts of uncontrolled violence, striking anything near it during combat. They are also capable of great speeds in combat, despite their large and cumbersome size. And lastly, any wound a clay golem inflicts remains open and debilitating until some kind of magical or supernatural healing is applied.

— Etahn

**Special Abilities**

**Berserk**
At the start of each combat round, there is a cumulative 1% chance that the golem's elemental spirit breaks free, causing it to attack the nearest living creature or object indiscriminately. Once berserk, it cannot be brought back under control.

**Cursed Wound**
Damage dealt by a clay golem cannot be healed naturally and resists magical healing. Casting a healing spell on a victim requires a *Difficult* Spellcraft check or the spell has no effect.

**Construct Traits**
Immune to disease, death effects, mind-affecting effects, paralysis, poison, sleep, stun, and any effect requiring a Fortitude save.

**Haste**
Once per day, after at least 1 round of combat, the clay golem can become hasted, allowing it to move at double speed and gain an extra attack.

**Immunity to Magic**
Certain spells affect the clay golem differently:

- *Bend Sound:* Does double damage.
- *Shape Earth and Stone:* Slows the golem by 1/2 and negates one attack.
