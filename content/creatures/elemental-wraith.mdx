- **Attack:** (2) Slam 75% 1d8+3+db+element
- **Skills:** Dodge 65%, Grapple 60%, Hide 60%, Listen 60%, Spot 60%, Stealth 60%, Other Skills 35%
- **Special:** Varies
- **Weaknesses:** Opposite Elements, Powerless

**Weakness Damage:**

Elemental Wraiths take double damage from attacks consisting of other elements, but are healed by their own elements.

**Weakness Powerless:**

If an opponent is connected to an opposing element, the wraiths attacks become *Difficult*. Example: a fighter steps into a knee deep creek while fighting an Air Wraith, so all attacks against the fighter become *Difficult*.

**Push:** A wraith can push a foe with enough force to knock them back 2d10 feet and do 1d6 damage. A successful Heroic Action using Effort must be made for the wraith to push an enemy. The target can be pushed in any direction away from the wraith.

**Envelope:** A wraith can try to envelope and hold an opponent with a successful, opposed Heroic Action using grapple. It must succeed at a Heroic Action for each round after that to keep the opponent enveloped. Each round the opponent is held the wraith causes an additional (1d6 + elemental) damage as a free action.

**Element Damage:** Attacks from the wraith also do elemental damage. This can be resisted with spells, magic items, or enchantments.

- **Earth:** 1d6 crushing damage
- **Air:** Knockback and 1d4 damage.
- **Water:** Suffocation&#42;. &#42;The water creeps into the target's body, causing them to not be able to breathe for 1 round, making all tasks difficult.
- **Fire:** 1d3 damage plus starts target on fire.
