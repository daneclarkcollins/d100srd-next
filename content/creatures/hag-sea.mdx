Sea hags haunt the coasts of Atheles. They lack the strong magical powers of other hags, but are so hideous they can distress any who gaze upon them. They are human-sized, around 5 to 6' tall, around 150 lbs.

They are solitary, staying near sparsely populated areas so when their victims disappear, there are few to gather and hunt them. Sea hags hunt and feed on anything, but relish the flesh of intelligent creatures.

—Etahn

### Special Abilities

**Amphibious**  
Although sea hags are aquatic, they can survive indefinitely on land.

**Evil Eye**  
3/day, gaze attack (30 ft.). Target must make a Spirit save or be dazed for 3 days (remove curse/dispel magic can cure sooner). Creatures immune to fear are unaffected.

**Horrific Appearance**  
Anyone who sees the hag must make a Stamnia save or suffer - 4 STR. Victims reduced to 0 STR are helpless. A creature that resists or is affected cannot be targeted again for 24 hours.
