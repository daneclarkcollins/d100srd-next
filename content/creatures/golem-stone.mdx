- **Attack:** Slam (x2) 85% 1d8+5+2d6 (19 average)
- **Skills:** Grapple 60%, Spot +60%
- **Special:** Slow (every 2 rounds), Tritium and Blackrock ignore AV, Construct Traits
- **Weaknesses:** Tritium, Blackrock

**Slow**  
Once every 2 rounds as a free action, the golem may unleash a wave of magical inertia. All creatures within 15 feet must make a *difficult* **Stamina check** or become **Slowed** for 2 rounds. Slowed creatures can only take 1 action per round and suffer -20% to defensive and attack rolls.

**Construct Traits**  
The golem is immune to critical hits, disease, death effects, mind-affecting effects, paralysis, poison, sleep, stun, and any effect that requires a Stamina check.

**Magic Effects**

- *Stone Shape*: Slows the golem (same as above) for 2d6 rounds. No resistance.
- *Soften Earth and Stone*: Negates the golem's damage reduction for 1 full round.
