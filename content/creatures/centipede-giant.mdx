From the dark crevice a large segmented insect lunged out, its long pincers coming together with a spine-chilling clacking. Or at least that is what I remember, the giant bug coming at me like it hadn’t eaten in months. Its bite connected and poisoned me. I encountered this abnormal vermin when I first started my career as a hunter. It was a close fight, its pincers leaving many scars, but my sword finally clove through its head, leaving it twitching in the mud of the old ruin.

- *Etahn*

While all sizes of centipedes are solitary, if an environment supports them, they can be a common occurrence.

- *Elstram’s Experiences with Wildlife*
