With hair like sagging grass and skin shining with slime over a sickly green, a green hag rises from the marshes to face her foe.

I am not sure what these creatures are meant to do within an environment, but a green hag is undoubtedly the worst part of a damp, murky, muddy, leech-filled swamp. They were named hags in the past as they resemble what we have feared and labeled witches within our own society. Having seen them up close I can say they have no sex, although they appear female, and I certainly have no idea how they reproduce, nor do I want to. They can alter their appearance, so when you meet that tavern maid in the swamp where maybe she shouldn't be, it's best to believe your intuition rather than your eyes.

They delight in savagery. Killing the young and the beautiful, torturing and teasing those they trap -- that's what they live for. Instead, give them something to die upon – a sword to their gut is the best way to end any meeting with a swamp hag.

—Etahn

### Special Abilities

**Weakening Touch**  
Once per round, as a free action, the hag makes a special touch attack; target must succeed on a Stamina save or suffer - 1d4 STR.

**Mimicry**  
A green hag can imitate the sound of any animal found near its lair with perfect accuracy.

**Resistant**  
A hag is naturally resistant to magical energy. This gives them 50% automatic damage reduction to magical AoE effects (no need for Save roll) or a 10% bonus to Saves vs Spells on single-target spells.

**Spell-Like Abilities** *(Mana costs in parentheses)*  
At will as a standard action - *acid fog (3), bend sound (3), spirit sap (3), transform (self only, 5)*

**Water Breathing**  
A green hag can breathe underwater as if it was air.

### Hag Covey (Optional)

When three hags of any type are within 10 ft. of each other, they may use the following as a group:  
3/day — *animate dead*, *bestow curse*, *dreamwalk*, *hold creature*, *hallucinatory terrain*, *nondetection*, *polymorph other*, *polymorph self*, *scrying*, *shape weather*.

### Hag Eye (Optional)

A covey can create one hag eye from a gem worth ≥ 20 gp. All hags in the covey can see through it at will. Destroying it deals 1d10 damage to each hag; the hag taking the most damage is blinded for 24 hours.
