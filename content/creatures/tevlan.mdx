The tevlani have been servants to the creatures of the Navirim for a million years. They are a short, gray or purple skinned race with spindly limbs.

They are highly intelligent and fast, though the illarth and ocuth have found ways to keep them in servitude.

A tevlan can use its move action to warp to any visible space within 40' as long as it is in the shadows. This is a magical effect, though it needs nothing other than the will of the tevlan.
