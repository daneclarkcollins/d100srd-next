A fey fox is a rare creature found in the wilderlands of the East. They are smart and cunning, although they little danger to the average traveller. They can track creatures who have escaped the Navirim, having a unique ability to see those who originate from its home plane. Because of this ability, many a hunter seek to train them as hunt animals, though elflings seem to be the only race who naturally bond with the creatures.

A fey fox is about 3 feet tall at the shoulders and 4 feet long, and weigh about 115 lbs.

— Etahn

**See Invisibility**
The fey fox can see invisible and ethereal creatures within its normal vision range, perceiving them as translucent. It can also see creatures in the In-Between, but not into the Navirim. This ability does not reveal illusions, allow vision through solid objects, or reveal hidden creatures.

**See Navirite**
The fey fox sees through all magical or mundane disguises of Navirites, revealing their true form regardless of appearance-altering effects.
