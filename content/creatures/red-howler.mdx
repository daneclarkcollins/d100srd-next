A large, four-legged creature stalks out from the shadows, its yellow cat-like eyes glowing in the dim light. Red barbs form a deadly mane around its head and run down its slender back. Its mouth parts in a growl, its white teeth stark against the black gums.

*—Elstram’s Experiences with Wildlife*

Red howlers find their way into Atheles through breaks in the Veil. They prowl the dark forests and wastes near where they came through. They are 8’ in length, weighing around 800 pounds. They have no language besides the growls of hunting, feeding, and mating.

When sighted or evidence is found of them in an area, a quick dispatch to a known hunter should be the first action. And then run.

—Etahn

**Quills**

Anyone who **attacks** the Red Howler with a **melee weapon (non-reach)**, natural weapon, or **unarmed strike** takes **1d4 piercing damage** as quills embed in their skin.
When the howler hits with its **bite**, the defender also suffers quill damage.
Removing embedded quills requires a **Difficult Survival check** (failure causes 1d4 additional damage).

**Howl**

As an action, the howler unleashes a terrifying howl.
All creatures within 15’ must succeed on a **Stamina check** or become **Dazed** for 1 round.

### Reagents and Remnants

#### Howler Quills *(Remnant)*

These blood-red barbed spines are prized by alchemists and dark fletchers. When carefully removed, they can be used to craft enchanted arrows or pain-inducing traps.

- *Use*: Weapon ammunition component (adds minor bleed effect 1d3), ritual focus for fear or pain magic
- *Value*: 30 gp per bundle (1d4 bundles)
- *Harvesting Difficulty*: **Average**

#### Vocal Core *(Reagent)*

The howler’s voicebox, known as the “vocal core,” retains residual magical resonance. It is sought after for crafting fear-inducing elixirs or enhancing sonic-based spells.

- *Use*: Alchemical fear potions, sound-amplifying enchantments
- *Value*: 75 gp
- *Harvesting Difficulty*: **Difficult**

#### Howler Hide *(Remnant)*

The tough, sinewy hide is dark red and warm to the touch. When tanned and treated, it retains mild vibration sensitivity and is sometimes used in stealth boots or monster-hide armor.

- *Use*: Light armor, stealth gear
- *Value*: 20 gp per hide (1 per creature)
- *Harvesting Difficulty*: **Average**
