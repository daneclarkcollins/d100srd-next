*Tentacus Scavana* is what the scholars call the tentacle crawler. Regardless of its fancy name, most care little for it or what it's called. This large, segmented insect, usually found in dark places, is feared across the world.

A tentacle crawler looks like a giant centipede with five octopus like tentacles emerging from its head. It roams the darkness in search of prey, using its tentacles to grapple it while it stings with its barbed tail. The best place to attack it is in the center, as far from either end as you can get, although it is quite capable of twisting to reach you wherever you slash it.

—Etahn

**Grab**

When an attack also does grab, the attacker and defender must roll a Heroic Action (tentacle crawler’s STR vs defender's STR or DEX. If the defender loses, they become grappled. They must succeed at a Heroic Action during their turn to free themselves.

A tentacle crawler can only hold one target. A grappled target is immobile and all attacks agauinst them are easy.
