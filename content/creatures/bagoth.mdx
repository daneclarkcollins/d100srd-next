Ba'goth’s are often used as soldiers by more powerful entities in the Navirim. They are the perfect soldiers: tough, immune to most damage, capable of multiple powerful attacks. During the Great War, the Uthgard Kingdom had a legion of ba'goth, controlled by a powerful Dreadlord mage.

When a ba'goth finds itself alone in our world, they often stalk a victim, relishing in its fear and distress. This has led to a folk tale view of them as boogeymen, hiding under beds or in closets to torment their victims. In truth, if a ba'goth stalks a person, that person will not likely last long.

—Oharad, *Cryptonombus of the Navirim*

- **Attack:** (2) claws 80% 1d6+4+dm
- **Skills:** Dodge 75%, Grapple 65%, Hide 65%, Listen 65%, Spot 65%, Stealth 65%
- **Special:** Darkvision 60’, dark, immune (acid, cold, and fire), protective slime, see invisible, sneak attack
- **Weaknesses:** Cold Iron
- **Habitat:** Any dark area.
- **Encounter:** Single, Pair, or Troop (4-20)

**Dark**

Once per day, a ba'goth can cause an area around it to become dark, as per a level 1 Dark spell.

**Protective Slime**

A layer of acidic slime coats a ba'goth’s skin. Any creature that strikes a ba'goth with a natural attack or unarmed strike takes 1d8 points of acid damage from this slime if it fails a DEX roll. A creature that strikes a ba'goth with a melee weapon must make a successful DEX roll or the weapon takes 1d8 points of acid damage. Ammunition that strikes a ba'goth is automatically destroyed after it inflicts its damage.

**Sneak Attack**

The ba'goth does an extra 2d6 points of damage whenever a foe is unaware of them, flanked, or prone.

**See Invisible**

A ba'goth can see anything that is hidden by a magical invisibility spell.

### Remnants and Reagents

**Ba'goth Acid (Remnant)**

The acid from the ba'goth can be harvested into vials. Each ba'goth has up to 6 doses of acid. This is Strong Acid, doing 1d10+1 or 1 AV for 10 rounds.

**Value:** 20 gp

**Harvesting Difficulty:** Average
