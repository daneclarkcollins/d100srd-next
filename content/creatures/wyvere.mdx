The so-called "false dragons" of the Steppes, the wyvere is a terror anywhere it is seen. The wyvere is large, covered in multicolored scales, and its long wings carry is far from its roost in the Steppelands.

*—Elstram’s Experiences with Wildlife*

The wyvere lives in the Steppes. Like the drake, it is believed to be a lesser cousin of the great dragons of old. It has only two legs and uses its front wings for stabilization while on the ground.

This creature weights less than it appears for its size, which is critical for its non-magical flight. Its scales are very tough and its hide is prized by hunters of rare game. The belly scales are almost as strong as metal and often large enough to be used as a breastplate. Wyvere eggs are also rumored to have medicinal qualities and are prized accordingly.

A wyvere is very light, and they need to stay that way. They use their acid spit to partially digest their prey before eating.

*—Zoological Encyclopedia*

### Special Abilities

**Acid Spit**

The wyvere can spit acidic venom 3/day as a ranged attack (40 ft). This venom is a sticky corrosive fluid that aids in digesting the intended victim prior to eating. Target takes **6d6 acid damage or their armor loses 2 AV for 1d4 rounds**. Target may **Dodge to evade.**

**Acidic Bite**

A successful bite deals an additional **2d6 acid damage** for **1d4 rounds**.

**Venom (Tail)**

If hit by the tail, the target must resist Wyvere venom.
