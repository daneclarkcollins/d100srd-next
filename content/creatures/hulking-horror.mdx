Hulking horrors are the bane of underground explorers. They would stand around 7' tall, if they stood fully erect, and weigh about 700 lbs. They have general appearance of a gorilla covered in bony plates, with large wide-set eyes, and two large insect-like mandibles on either side of its mouth.

They are incredibly strong and resilient, and one must be careful not to become caught between those powerful jaws – death usually follows quickly if that happens.

—Etahn

**Mandible Grapple**

Hulking horrors can grapple with their large mandibles as well as their hands, enabling them to make two Heroic Actions in a round to grapple two different opponents or the same opponent twice. Grapples made at a 10-foot reach are always possible with the mandibles.

**Tremorsense**

A creature with tremorsense automatically senses the location of anything that is in contact with the ground and within range. Aquatic creatures with tremorsense can also sense the location of creatures moving through water.

If no straight path exists through the ground from the creature to those that it's sensing, then the range defines the maximum distance of the shortest indirect path. It must itself be in contact with the ground, and the creatures must be moving.
