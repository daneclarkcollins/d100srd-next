A pale creature, its skin thin as parchment, with long fingernails curled like blackened claws. It jumps at you with its mouth wide open, showing blackened pointed teeth.

Ghasts are creatures who are undead, living among the dead and eating their rotting flesh. It is believed the first ghasts came from cannibalistic humanoids, who carried their hunger for human flesh into death.

A hunter can find many contracts around larger cities to keep their graveyards free of ghasts, as they have become something of an epidemic since magic has returned. You must be quick, and stay free of its bite, as it can paralyze even the strongest warriors.

— Etahn

**Paralyze**
