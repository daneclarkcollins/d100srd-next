A dretch is a common creature from the Navirim, often seen entering the lands near portal stones or places deep in the earth. What purpose they serve in the Navirim, I do not know, as they seem uninterested or unaware of what happens around them.

They are only around 4 feet tall, and sometimes almost as wide. Pale, translucent skin hangs in folds, their eyes just as pale and blank. Their skin is tough as leather and their yellowed claws and teeth are hard as steel.

—Oharad, *Cryptonombus of the Navirim*.

**Belching Acid**

Once per day, as a free action, the dretch can belch a cloud of acid that surrounds it in a 5’ radius. The fog lasts four rounds and follows the dretch. It is considered standard acid, doing 1d6+1 damage to flesh and 1 AV reduction per round.

### Remnants and Reagents

A dretch's acid gland can be removed, which holds two doses of standard acid.
