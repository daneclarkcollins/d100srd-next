Frost giants are the most feared denizens of the far north. They are much larger than their other cousins, save for the massive mountain giant. They bring destruction on land or at sea. Born with innate magic talent, and many among them are learned spellcasters. They adorn themselves with tattoos, and the mages among them often tattoo their spellbooks on their skin. They can not use iron or steel, so amarnium and tritium are greatly prized by them.

Frost giants are a dying race. They have strange growths and mutations, passed down along their family trees. Despite the careful care and attention of the parents, most die before adolescence—the reasons for these inherited illnesses remain a mystery. They are immune to the cold, and can hold their breath for long periods of time, though fire and heat do great harm to them.

They have a matriarchal society, with the ability of childbearing one of the most important abilities of a frost giant. Tribes are usually run by an elected female, chosen after long periods of meditation and "dream walking," or the power to have lucid dreams. The power and symbology of their dreams holds an importance to frost giants that cannot be understated.

They have grey skin with white or blonde hair. They stand around 18 feet tall and weigh around 9,000 lbs.
