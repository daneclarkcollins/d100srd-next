*First, you hear the clacking, as if metal spears are being jabbed into stone and the sound reverberates down the hallway. Then you see it: a metal monstrosity, half spider and half furnace, as it turns its empty, glowing eyes your way. Tubes and claws swing toward you, and its metal legs clank and slam into the floor as it approaches you*

While a dworven drone is not a real creature, it is something I have come across in multiple forms. The dworves of old created their constructs to perform various jobs, often with a secondary task of guarding their surroundings. Some still follow ancient dworven commands, but many have long broken free of anything but their daily routine.

— Etahn

From whence comes the dworven drone's animating force? The gods never breathed life or soul into them. They cannot act outside of the routines given them by their dworven creators. If you cut through one's metal exterior, it does not bleed, except for some gooey black substance that courses through its structure, some strange substance the dworves drew from the deeps beneath their mountains.

These things do not have a part in the cycle of life, and as such, should be destroyed.

— Delgon

- **Habitat:** Any

**Special Abilities**

**Flame-throw**
Emits fire in a 15 ft. cone. Deals 1d6+1 fire damage. Targets may Dodge (Heroic Action vs 60) to halve damage.

**Grease Spray**
As the Grease spell. Covers 15 ft. cone. Anyone in area must make a Dodge (Heroic Action vs 55) or fall prone.

**Self-Destruct**
When reduced to 0 HP, the drone begins to tick. After 20 seconds (2 rounds), it explodes:

- Fire Blast (10 ft. radius): 1d8+1 fire (Dodge vs 60)
- Shrapnel Burst (20 ft. radius): 1d6+2 physical (Dodge vs 60)

**Command Override**
If addressed in Dworven and the speaker succeeds on a Knowledge check (DC 20), the following commands can be issued (Will Save/Stamina vs 60 to resist):

- Stop – Drone halts and awaits orders.
- Clean – Drone disengages and begins maintenance.
- Sleep – Drone powers down.

### Remnants and Reagents

**Arcane Crystal Core (Reagent)**
A small, humming crystal that powered the drone's core routines. Can be recharged for use in magical constructs or unstable fire-based devices.
Value: 50 gp
Harvesting Difficulty: Difficult

**Shrapnel Plate Shards (Remnant)**
Jagged pieces of outer casing, often warped from self-destruct damage. Useful in crafting shrapnel-based traps or as raw material for low-grade armor.
Value: 10 gp per shard (1d4 shards)
Harvesting Difficulty: Average
