Of all the creatures I have faced, the lich is the one who has left the deepest scar. And it escaped, to who knows where.

Liches are undead mages, who, through unspeakable rituals and necromantic magic, have extended their life beyond the natural. Delgon and I had tracked a number of supernatural occurrences to a hideaway deep in the wastes. There, beyond traps, minions, and demons, we found the lich. He had existed in this stone fortress for an untold amount of time. The corpses and body parts of various animals and sentient creatures of Atheles littered its laboratory, a ghastly sight and an indescribably horrible stench. Delgon and I had seen many incredible and impossible things in our years of hunting, but this leather-skinned corpse, its red eyes glowing with malevolent intelligence and utter hatred shook us to our core. We fought him for hours, and finally destroyed the unnatural thing… or so we thought.

But several months later, more occurrences of the supernatural occurred, and one night, in my sleep the lich appeared above my bed. He reached down, placing a cold hand on my chest and I felt my heart seize. As my vision faded to black, I heard his laughing voice gloatingly taunt "You shall slip to dust and worms while I still roam these lands..."

I woke to Delgon's worried face, his healing magics having brought me back. Still to this day when I feel a pain in my chest I feel that cold grip and a deathly chill runs throughout my entire body.

Where that cursed creature went we may never know, as his fortress was empty when we returned weeks later.

—Etahn

### Special Abilities

**Energy Burst**  
At will. Fires a 40 ft. ranged orb of elemental energy (fire, ice, acid, stone, or air), dealing 1d4+1 per 2 caster levels (8d4+8).

**Energy Blast**  
3/day. Ranged 40 ft. orb that explodes in 15 ft. radius, dealing 4d4 energy damage (chosen element) or healing 4d4 to undead.

**Energy Wall**  
1/day. Creates a 15x15x2 ft. wall of energy (chosen element) for 2d4 rounds. Crossing deals 6d4 damage.

**Call of the Elements**  
1/day. Summons elemental storm in 40 ft. radius. Deals 8d6+8 damage, Reflex save halves, or Stuns all creatures (Will save negates).

**Paralyzing Touch**  
Any living creature struck by melee attack must succeed on a Stamina save or be paralyzed for 24 hours.

**Fear Aura**  
Creatures within 60 ft. must make a Spirit save or suffer *Fear* (Sanity 1/1d6). Those who succeed are immune to that lich's aura for 24 hours.

**Ravaging**  
Liches ravage to fuel greater spellcasting, sacrificing life around them.

**Spell Sight**  
Can memorize a spell witnessed being cast.

**Undead Traits**  
Immune to disease, poison, mind-affecting spells, sleep, paralysis, cold, electricity, and *mage bolt*.

**Immortal (Arcanima)**  
The lich is bound to a magical object (arcanima). If destroyed, it reforms in 1d10 days unless the arcanima is destroyed.

### Creating a Lich (SagaBorn D100)

When a powerful spellcaster performs the horrific rituals of lichdom, their soul is bound to an **Arcanima**. The transformation alters their body and abilities permanently.

**Challenge Value**  
All liches are considered Boss Tier; therefore, they increase their **CV by +3**.

**Size and Type**  
Type changes to **Undead**. Size is unchanged.

**Hit Points**  
Undead creatures do not use CON for HP, instead using SIZ x2. Since they are Bosses, their HP is then multiplied by x3.

**Armor Value**  
The lich's natural Armor Value improves by **+5**, reflecting hardened flesh and magical defenses.

**Attacks**

- Retains all base creature attacks.
- Gains a **Touch attack**: 70% chance to hit, deals **1d8+CV negative energy damage**, which heals undead creatures.
- Gains **Paralyzing Touch**: Target must make a **Stamina save** or be paralyzed for 24 hours.

**Sanity / Horror**

- First Encounter: **1/1d8** Horror.
- **Fear Aura:** 1/1d6 Horror (see below).

**Special Abilities**

- **Fear Aura:** All creatures within 60 ft. must make a **Spirit save** or be affected as if under a *Fear* spell. Successful saves grant immunity to that lich's aura for 24 hours.
- **Paralyzing Touch:** As above.
- **Spells:** A lich retains all spellcasting from life. Gains +30 Mana.
- **Damage Resistance:** DR 12/Legacy &amp; Magic.
- **Immunities:** Immune to cold, electricity, *mage bolt*, and mind-affecting spells.
- **Undead Traits:** Immune to disease, poison, paralysis, sleep, and effects requiring Fortitude saves.
- **Immortality:** Cannot be slain unless its **Arcanima** is destroyed.

**Abilities**

- **+2 INT, +2 ACU, +2 SOC**
- **CON removed** (undead have no Constitution).

**Treasure**  
Double. A lich typically has **Legacy Items** appropriate to its level and status.

**Weaknesses**

- **Arcanima:** Unless destroyed, the lich reforms 1d10 days after apparent destruction.
- **Legacy Weapons / Magic:** Only Legacy or magical weapons bypass its full Damage Resistance.

**Level Advancement**  
Liches cannot progress as living spellcasters beyond level 8, but can advance as **Undead**:

- +1d12 HP per level
- +1 to one save (choice)
- +1 Base Attack
- +1 Skill Point (max rank 5)
- +1 Talent Point every 2 levels
