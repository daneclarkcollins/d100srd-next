Minotaurs are a species of beings who are said to have been brought to Atheles from over the sea. They are easily angered and a cunning foe.

They stand over 7 feet tall and weigh about 400 lbs, with a bull-like head and horns that they use to charge and gore their opponents.

*—Creaturi Obscuria*

### Special Abilities

**Charge**

A minotaur may use both of its actions to charge a total of 40’ and then use Powerful Gore against one target.

**Powerful Charge**

When charging, a minotaur may use its horns in a devastating gore attack: Gore 75% 4d6+3+2d6.

**Natural Cunning**

Minotaurs cannot become lost, are immune to *maze* effects, and are almost never caught flat-footed. They are adept trackers, using both instinct and logic.

**Track**

Can follow trails using Track.

**Scent**

A creature with scent can smell creatures within 30’ even if they are hidden or invisible. They may use an *easy* Spot check to detect creatures nearby by their scent.
