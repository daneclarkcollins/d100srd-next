- **Attack:** Claw (2) 85% 2d10+2+2d6 (Slashing) and Kick 75% 2d12+2+2d6 (Crushing, Knockback DC 18)
- **Skills:** Combat 85%, Intimidate 65%, Endurance 65%, Awareness 55%, Command 75%, Spot 70%, Stealth 25%, Other Skills 45%
- **Special:** Darkvision 90’, Hivemind (with all orlocks in 1 mile), Queen’s Pheromones, Pheromone Cloud, Hive Control

**Queen’s Pheromones (Free Action):**

Once per round, she may target up to **two creatures** within 30’. Each must make a contested difficult **Spirit Save** or fall under her psychic influence. While influenced, the queen can implant a command (like *Command* spell). She can **only maintain one dominated target** at a time. Attempting a second control breaks the first. The controlled creature may attempt to resist at the start of each turn.

**Pheromone Cloud (Aura):**

All non-orlocks within 10’ must make a contested **Stamina Save** each round or suffer: **1d4 damage** from choking and **–10% to all actions** for 1 round.

**Hive Control:**

An orlock queen can issue simple commands to orlocks within range without using actions.

### Reagents and Remnants

**Pheromone Gland** (Reagent)
A pulsing sac located behind the Queen’s throat, this gland exudes potent hormonal vapors. Alchemists covet it for charm elixirs, while summoners prize its ability to bind lesser minds.
**Use:** Ingredient for charm/command potions, Hivecaller bombs, or pheromone-based rituals
**Value:** 300 gp
**Harvesting Difficulty:** Difficult

**Queen’s Carapace Fragment** (Remnant)
Thick, interlocking chitin plates from the Queen’s torso are incredibly strong and laced with dark resin. They are rarely intact after battle but can be repurposed into powerful armor.
**Use:** Crafting component for high-end chitin armor or shields (AV 8)
**Value:** 1000 gp per fragment (1d2 can be harvested)
**Harvesting Difficulty:** Very Difficult

**Royal Ichor** (Reagent)
This viscous, dark purple fluid flows through the Queen’s organs. It pulses with arcane resonance and a faint heat. Often used by ravagers or dark alchemists.
**Use:** Base for advanced mana regeneration potions or anti-corruption salves
**Value:** 50 gp per vial (1d4 vials)
**Harvesting Difficulty:** Average

**Unfertilized Egg Cluster** (Remnant)
These translucent eggs glow faintly and contain inert larvae. Though not viable for spawning, they radiate hive essence.
**Use:** Catalyst in summoning rituals, sacrificial magic, or fertility curses
**Value:** 75 gp per intact cluster
**Harvesting Difficulty:** Very Difficult

**Nerve Node** (Reagent)
These twitching nodes, no larger than a walnut, relay the Queen’s will through the hive. They are psycho-reactive and sensitive to magical fields.
**Use:** Spell component for mind-affecting spells (Command, Fear, Confusion)
**Value:** 125 gp each (1d3 found)
**Harvesting Difficulty:** Difficult

**Hive Dust** (Remnant)
A strange, glittering dust found clinging to the Queen’s underside and lair surroundings. It smells of spice and decay.
**Use:** Low-level illusion, misdirection, or masking powders
**Value:** 100 gp per pouch
**Harvesting Difficulty:** Easy
