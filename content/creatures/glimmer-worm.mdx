The glimmer worm is a predator deep in the caverns of Atheles. Its name comes from the way its filaments can glimmer in strong torchlight. It nests in the upper alcoves of tall caves or rooms, laying its sticky strands from the ceiling which drape down to the ground. It waits in silence until something gets caught, then it moves quickly to reel in its catch.

Bones, carapaces, and other pieces of leftover prey litter the floor below a glimmer worm. Though often unseen, they are a frightful sight. Slick, pale flesh covers its body, and large white orbs, which may have once served as eyes, sit atop its fish-like head. The same sticky residue found on its filament exudes from its skin, allowing it to climb walls and ceilings. If feeds through a large gaping orifice of a mouth surrounded by suckers, which help it latch on to prey while it feeds.

A glimmer worm can grow to over 8 feet long and weigh as much as 200 pounds.

*—Zoological Encyclopedia*

- **Attack:** Bite 1d8+2+dm
- **Skills:** Combat and Main Skills 60%, Other Skills 35%
- **Special:** Filament, Pull, Hold
- **Weaknesses:** Fire burns its filaments.

**Filament**

A glimmer worm drapes its lair with silk-like filaments. The strands are thin and transparent, needing a Spot check to see them. A creature who accidentally touches a glimmer worm's filament becomes attached to the sticky thread. As a standard action, a creature can rip the filament free with a *difficult* Effort check. A caught creature can also attempt to escape a filament by making a *difficult* Acrobatics check. A filament has 10 hit points, and has DR 15/slashing. An application of liquid with high alcohol content (or a dose of universal solvent) dissolves the adhesive and releases the creature caught by the filament. A glimmer worm can have up to twelve filaments extruding at a time.

**Pull**

Once a target is stuck in a filament, the glimmer worm reels the target into its maw. It must succeed at a Heroic Action (Effort vs Effort) to pull the target at 30 feet per round towards its mouth. The glimmer worm gets a single bite attack the same round it successfully pulls the target to its mouth.

**Hold**

A target that is successfully pulled to the glimmer worm becomes grappled. Each round while held, a trapped adventurer may attempt a Heroic Action (Effort vs Effort) on their turn to try and free themselves.

### Reagents and Remnants

The glimmer worm's silk gland is filled with the strong filament they string in their lairs. It is worth 200 gp. Silk from a Glimmer Worm can be used to string a bow, which gives it +2 to damage.
