Frost worms are giant carnivorous creatures who live in the far north. They can burrow through the snow and ice and even swim in the frigid waters of the northern sea. Young ones are 40 feet long and 5 feet or more in diameter, and they grow much larger as they age. They lie in wait while hunting, using a stunning shriek to stun their prey before they attack with their powerful jaws.

*—Creaturi Obscuria*

**SIZ Note**

Gigantic (SIZ 55). Frost worms occupy a massive space and have a Reach of 25 feet with their Bite and Slam attacks.

**Cold**

A frost worm's body radiates supernatural cold. Its Bite and Slam attacks inflict an additional 1d8 Cold damage.

Any creature striking a frost worm with an unarmed attack or natural weapon immediately suffers 1d8 Cold damage.

**Trill**

As a Free Action, the frost worm emits a haunting, resonating trill. All non-frost worms within 100 feet must succeed at a Difficult Stamina check or become Stunned for as long as the worm continues trilling and for 1d4 rounds afterward.

A stunned creature that is attacked or shaken violently may immediately attempt another Stamina check.

Once a creature successfully resists or recovers from a particular frost worm's Trill, it is immune to that worm's Trill for 24 hours.

A frost worm cannot use its Breath Weapon while Trilling.

**Breath Weapon**

Once per hour, a frost worm exhales a 30-foot cone of freezing mist.

Creatures within the cone suffer 10d6 Cold damage. A successful Agility (DEX) Save halves the damage.

Creatures currently stunned by Trill receive no Save against the Breath Weapon.

**Death Throes**

When slain, a frost worm explodes into a storm of frozen shards.

Every creature within 100 feet suffers 6d6 Cold damage and 8d6 Piercing damage.

A successful DEX Save halves both damage types.

**Darkvision**

A frost worm can see normally in darkness out to 60 feet, provided there is at least the faintest available light.

**Immunity to Cold**

A frost worm is completely immune to Cold damage and all magical or mundane effects based on extreme cold.

**Vulnerability to Fire**

Fire attacks inflict an additional +2d6 Fire damage against a frost worm after all other damage has been calculated.

### Reagents and Remnants

**Frost Worm Heart**

The massive heart of a frost worm radiates supernatural cold for weeks after the creature's death. Master alchemists prize it for brewing powerful frost oils, cold wards, and enchantments.

Value: 1,200 gp

Harvesting Difficulty: Very Difficult

**Icefang Shards**

The worm's crystalline teeth and jaw fragments remain harder than steel after death. Smiths craft them into masterwork arrowheads, spear tips, and cold-forged blades.

Value: 400 gp

Harvesting Difficulty: Difficult

**Frostscale Hide**

The worm's thick, frost-infused hide can be crafted into exceptional armor that protects against extreme cold and harsh environments.

Value: 900 gp

Harvesting Difficulty: Very Difficult
