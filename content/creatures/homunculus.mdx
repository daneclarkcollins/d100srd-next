A small construct created by a wizard to aid them as they see fit. They vary in appearance and size, depending on the powers given to them by their creator. Unlike other constructs, the wizard is bonded to this particular creation, as they imbue a small part of their own spirit to animate the homunculus.

*—Dazerum Colthax, Scrivener of Eredar*

### Special Abilities

**Summoner's Bond**  
A homunculus is bound to its creator, who invests some of their own spirit to animate it. The bond grants scaling powers:

- **Link (Talent Level 3):** Creator can command telepathically as a free action.
- **Evasion (Talent Level 4):** On a successful Agility save vs. area attacks, the homunculus takes no damage.
- **Share Vision (Talent Level 8):** Creator can see through its eyes at any distance.

**Imbued Trait**  
During creation, the caster sacrifices 1–2 Characteristic points, granting the homunculus corresponding traits. These may include enhanced STR/DEX/CON, flying speed, languages, persuasion, or even cuteness-based influence (*Adorable*). If the homunculus dies, the caster regains lost Characteristics in 1d4 months.

**Adorable (SOC Imbued)**  
Some homunculi radiate an unnatural cuteness. Anyone seeing it must succeed on a Heroic Action (SOC vs SOC) or be favorably inclined toward it, potentially hesitating to harm it or showing it undue trust.
