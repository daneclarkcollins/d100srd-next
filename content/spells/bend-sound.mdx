---
title: "Bend Sound"
order: 0
category: "spell"
description: "Bend Sound spell for SagaBorn D100"
---

*   Mana per Level: Varies
*   Casting Time: 1 Standard Action
*   Range: Varies
*   Target: Varies
*   Duration: 1 round per level
*   Avoidance: Dodge for half unless otherwise stated
*   Special: Double damage. Critical: Double damage maxxed.
*   Type: Damage

**Mana 1**: You produce a loud, piercing sound that can break fragile, non-magical items, temporarily deafen an opponent, or harm creatures made of crystal.

When used in an area, this effect shatters non-magical objects made of crystal, glass, ceramic, or porcelain within a 5-foot radius. These objects are broken into pieces unless they weigh more than one pound per your mage's Talent Point Level. All exposed fragile or crystalline objects and crystalline creatures also take 1d6 sonic damage.

You can also deafen one target for 2d4 rounds with a successful attack. The target can attempt a CON save to halve the duration.

**Mana 3**: You can manipulate sound to counter and dispel a *Silence* spell by making a Heroic action against the opposing caster's *Spellcraft* skill.

**Mana 5**: You unleash a powerful blast of sound across a 30’ area, causing each creature in it to suffer 3d8 sonic damage. A successful CON save is required to avoid being stunned for 1 round. Creatures that are deaf cannot be stunned but still take damage. All exposed fragile or crystalline objects and crystalline creatures also take 1d6 sonic damage. This effect does not penetrate a *Silence* spell; to bypass it, the *Silence* spell must be dispelled first.

**Mana 7**: You release a devastating, high-pitched scream that deafens and injures all creatures in its 60’ cone range. Affected creatures are deafened for 2d6 rounds and take 5d6 sonic damage, with a Acumen save to resist deafness and Dodge to halve the damage. All exposed fragile or crystalline objects and crystalline creatures also take 1d6 sonic damage. This effect also cannot penetrate a *Silence* spell and requires dispelling the silence to take effect fully.

Spellcraft failure: The spell fizzles and the mana is lost.
