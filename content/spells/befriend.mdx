---
title: "Befriend"
order: 0
category: "spell"
description: "Befriend spell for SagaBorn D100"
---

*   Mana per Level: 3
*   Casting Time: 1 Standard Action
*   Range: 30’
*   Target: Single Target
*   Duration: 1 Hour per ACU
*   Avoidance: Contested Roll; Acumen vs. Acumen
*   Special: Double Duration
*   Type: Mind

The target sees the spellcaster as a friend. The target will engage with them in a friendly manner, but will not do anything that would harm themselves or others they consider allies. Attempts to *Persuade* them become Easy, unless it is dangerous or against their beliefs. They are still fully capable of their own actions and thoughts and lose no memories of past interactions with the spellcaster or their allies.

Spellcraft failure: The spell fizzles and the mana is lost.
