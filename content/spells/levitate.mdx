# Levitate

*   Mana per Level: 1
*   Casting Time: 1 Standard Action
*   Range: 30’ per mana
*   Target: Single Target
*   Duration: 2 minutes per mana
*   Avoidance: Contested Roll Spirit vs. Spirit
*   Special:
*   Type: Utility

Levitate makes a creature or object levitate. It takes one mana per 10 SIZ to levitate. The target may be raised or lowered 30’ per mana each round. The spellcaster most concentrate on this task. While the spellcaster can not move the target horizontally, the target may pull itself along a surface, such as a wall, to also change their position as they rise or lower.

Attacking while levitating is Difficult. If the spell ends the target floats slowly to the ground.

Spellcraft failure: The spell fizzles, and mana is lost.
