# Seal

*   Mana per Level: 1
*   Casting Time: 1 Standard Action
*   Range: 60’
*   Target: Single Target
*   Duration: 15 minutes per mana
*   Avoidance: Dodge
*   Special: Doubles the duration
*   Type: Utility

You magically seal the edges of two inanimate objects. These objects must not be in use, and fitted against one another. For example a door and a door frame, or a sword and this sheath. This bonds the two items as if one, and can not be separated unless by more powerful magic. Items may still be battered and broken, but the spell also gives them an additional 20 HP.

Each level adds either 15 minutes to the duration of the spell or adds +20 additional hit points to the hit points of the sealed objects. The caster may split levels between these two benefits, deciding how before casting the spell.

Spellcraft failure: The spell fizzles, and mana is lost.
