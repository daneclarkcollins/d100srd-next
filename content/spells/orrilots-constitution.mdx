# Orrilot’s Constitution

*   Mana per Level: 2
*   Casting Time: 1 Standard Action
*   Range: Touch
*   Target: Single Target
*   Duration: 1 minute
*   Avoidance: None
*   Special: Double the duration
*   Type: Buff

The target gains more Constitution. 

*   2 Mana: +10 to CON
*   4 Mana: +20 to CON
*   6 Mana: +30 to CON

Spellcraft failure: The spell fizzles, and mana is lost.
