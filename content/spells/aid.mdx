description: "Aid spell for SagaBorn D100"
---

*   Mana per Level: 1
*   Casting Time: 1 Standard Action
*   Range: Touch
*   Target: Single Target
*   Duration: 1 minute
*   Avoidance: None
*   Special: Doubles Bonus
*   Type: Buff

The target gains +10 per mana spent on a chosen skill until that skill is used.

Spellcraft failure: The spell fizzles and the mage loses the mana spent.
