# Animal Friend

*   Mana per Level: 1
*   Casting Time: 1 Standard Action
*   Range: 60’
*   Target: Single Target
*   Duration: 1 minute per mana
*   Avoidance: Spirit
*   Special:
*   Type: Utility

You may use Persuasion with animals. Each level of mana allows you +10% to your Persuasion skill when dealing with animals. The communication between the mage and the animal is simple, with emotions or simple directions from the caster being understood by the animal. The caster can also gain simple understanding of the animal's wants or needs.

Spellcraft failure: The spell fizzles, and mana is lost.
