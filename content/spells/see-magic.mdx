# See Magic

*   Mana per Level: 1
*   Casting Time: 1 Standard Action
*   Range: 60’
*   Target: Single Target
*   Duration: Concentration
*   Avoidance: None
*   Special: Doubles the range.
*   Type: Utility

You see any magic items or spells active in the current range. The mana level determines the level of detail you have in your magic sight. The spell can not see through iron, one foot of stone, three feet of wood, or any other magical ways of hiding magic.

*   1 Mana: You will detect a faint aura in a general area where magic is.
*   2 Mana: The aura for magic becomes stronger, showing the exact area where magic is present.
*   3 Mana: With a Knowledge Arcana check the mage may learn some basic facts about the magic detected.
*   4 Mana: The same as the 2 mana version, except the spell lasts 1 hour without concentration.

Spellcraft failure: The spell fizzles, and mana is lost.
