title: "Find"
order: 0
category: "spell"
description: "Find spell for SagaBorn D100"
---

*   Mana: 5
*   Casting Time: 1 hour
*   Range: 15 miles
*   Target: Single Target
*   Duration: Instant
*   Avoidance: None
*   Special: None
*   Type: Utility

This spell helps you locate a person or object familiar to you. As you turn, you sense when you are facing the direction of the target, provided it is within range. If the target is moving, you also know its direction of travel.

The spell can find a specific creature, a known item, or an item of a particular kind. However, it cannot locate creatures of a general type. To locate a kind of creature, you must have seen one up close (within 30 feet) at least once.

Spellcraft failure: The spell fizzles, and mana is lost.
