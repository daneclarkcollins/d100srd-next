---
title: "Blur"
order: 0
category: "spell"
description: "Blur spell for SagaBorn D100"
---

*   Mana per Level: Varies
*   Casting Time: 1 Standard Action
*   Range: Touch
*   Target: Single Target
*   Duration: 1 minute
*   Avoidance: None
*   Special: Double Duration
*   Type: Buff

4 mana: The spell’s target shifts in this plane, making them harder to see and touch. Any attack against the target becomes difficult.

7 mana: The spell’s target becomes difficult to see, making attacks against them difficult, as well as allowing the blurred creature to move through most solid objects. Iron or steel block blurred creatures.

Spellcraft failure: The spell fizzles and mana is lost.
