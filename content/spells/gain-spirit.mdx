order: 0
category: "spell"
description: "Gain Spirit spell for SagaBorn D100"
---

*   Mana per Level: 3
*   Casting Time: 1 Standard Action
*   Range: Touch
*   Target: Single Target
*   Duration: Instant
*   Avoidance: None
*   Special: Doubles spirit
*   Type: Buff

The target recovers 1 lost Spirit Point per 3 mana spent.

Spellcraft failure: The spell fizzles and doesn’t work.
