---
title: "Imbue Staff"
order: 0
category: "spell"
description: "Imbue Staff spell for SagaBorn D100"
---

*   Mana per Level: Varies
*   Casting Time: 2 minutes
*   Range: Touch
*   Target: Self
*   Duration: 4 hours
*   Avoidance: None
*   Special: Doubles duration
*   Type: Buff

Preferred by the druids of the Druid Council, this causes their weapon to become a powerful way to subdue an enemy without lethal force.

*   1 Mana: The spellcaster’s staff gains +5% to hit and +1d4 subdual damage.
*   3 Mana: The spellcaster’s staff gains +10% to hit and +2d4 subdual damage.
*   5 Mana: The spellcaster’s staff gains +15% to hit and +3d4 subdual damage.

Spellcraft failure: The spell fizzles, and mana is lost.
