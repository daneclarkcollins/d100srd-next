# Ward

*   Mana per Level: 1
*   Casting Time: 1 Standard Action
*   Range: Touch
*   Target: Single Target or Space
*   Duration: 2 hours per mana
*   Avoidance: None
*   Special: Doubles Duration
*   Type: Utility

You create a warded sphere 10’ x 10’ by touching it. Anything that moves through the warded space can do one of the following:

A loud noise, such as a wail or scream can be heard for all within 100’.

The spellcaster is alerted silently to the ward being disturbed.

The spellcaster can designate people or items that will not trigger the alarm.

Spellcraft failure: The spell fizzles, and mana is lost.
