# Cure Disease

*   Mana per Level: 1
*   Casting Time: 1 hour
*   Range: Touch
*   Target: Single Target
*   Duration: Instant
*   Avoidance: N/A
*   Special: Double the bonus
*   Type: Healing

Cure disease gives the target a +10% to their Stamina the next time they save versus a disease. For each mana level spent, the spell adds an additional +10% to the save.

Spellcraft failure: The spell fizzles, and mana is lost.
