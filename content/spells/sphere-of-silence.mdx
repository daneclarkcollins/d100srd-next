---
title: "Sphere of Silence"
order: 0
category: "spell"
description: "Sphere of Silence spell for SagaBorn D100"
---

*   Mana per Level: 1
*   Casting Time: 1 Standard Action
*   Range: 60’
*   Target: 10’ Spherical Space
*   Duration: 1 minute per mana level
*   Avoidance: *Difficult* Acumen Save
*   Special: Doubles duration Critical: save is *Very difficult*
*   Type: Utility

A sphere of space negates all sound within. No words can be spoken, verbal spells will not work, footfalls are silent. If one wishes to make a sound, they must make a successful save.

Spellcraft failure: The spell fizzles, and mana is lost.
