# Push

*   Mana per Level: 1
*   Casting Time: 1 Standard Action
*   Range: 60’
*   Target: Single Target
*   Duration: Instant
*   Avoidance: Agility or Effort
*   Special:
*   Type: Utility

A magical force pushes the object. This can be a person or an object. At mana level 1 it can cause Knockback against a target creature or knock over an object 5 lbs. or smaller.

Each additional mana can push a person or object 40 lbs. heavier 10’ backwards, doing 1d6 damage per mana. Avoidance with Agility or Effort stops the creature from being pushed, and they only take 1/2 damage.

Spellcraft failure: The spell fizzles, and mana is lost.
