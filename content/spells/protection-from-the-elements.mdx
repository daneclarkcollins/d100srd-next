# Protection from the Elements

*   Mana per Level: 1
*   Casting Time: 1 Standard Action
*   Range: Touch
*   Target: Single Target
*   Duration: 10 minutes per mana
*   Avoidance: Spirit
*   Special: Doubles duration
*   Type: Buff

The target becomes immune to a specific element (fire, water, ice/cold, earth, air, or electricity) for 10 damage.

Each mana gives 10 minutes to duration and blocks an additional 10 damage.

Spellcraft failure: The spell fizzles, and mana is lost.
