---
title: "Resist"
order: 0
category: "spell"
description: "Resist spell for SagaBorn D100"
---

*   Mana per Level: 1
*   Casting Time: 1 Standard Action
*   Range: Touch
*   Target: Single Target
*   Duration: 1 minute mana
*   Avoidance: None
*   Special: Double Duration
*   Type: Buff

Target is imbued with a +5% bonus to any Save for the duration. Each mana level gives an additional 5% **or** an additional target.

Spellcraft failure: The spell fizzles, and mana is lost.
