# Slow

*   Mana: 3
*   Casting Time: 1 Standard Action
*   Range: 60’
*   Target: Single Target
*   Duration: 1d4 rounds
*   Avoidance: Contested Roll Spirit vs. Spirit
*   Special: Save is _difficult_
*   Type: Debuff

The target becomes slowed. A slowed creature moves at 1/2 speed and loses half of their attacks (rounded up). A creature may attempt a save as a Move or Standard actions once per round after the first.

Spellcraft failure: The spell fizzles, and mana is lost.
