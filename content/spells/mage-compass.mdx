---
title: "Mage Compass"
order: 0
category: "spell"
description: "Mage Compass spell for SagaBorn D100"
---

*   Mana per Level: 1
*   Casting Time: 1 Standard Action
*   Range: 60’
*   Target: Single Target
*   Duration: Instant
*   Avoidance: None
*   Special: Adds +2 to the bonus. Critical: Doubles the bonus.
*   Type: Utility

Mage compass bestows directional sense to it’s target. This gives a 5% bonus to the spell target in Navigate skill checks per mana level.

Spellcraft failure: The spell fizzles, and mana is lost.
