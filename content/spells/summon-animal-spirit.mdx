*   Mana per Level: 2
*   Casting Time: 1 Standard Action
*   Range: 30’
*   Target: An empty 5’ space
*   Duration: 1 minute per mana
*   Avoidance: None
*   Special: Gains 1d6 HP
*   Type: Summoning

Summons a animal spirit to fight and complete tasks. A summoned animal spirit takes the form of any known animal. They have the same physical characteristics of the chosen animal, but their SIZ is based on the mana spent. Spending 2 mana summons a spirit of SIZ 4, CV 1 animal&#42; (based on the Creature Compendium). This increases by 1 CV per 2 mana spent.

&#42;A summoned animal spirit can be Tiny or Fine, but will also have the stats of the smaller creature.
