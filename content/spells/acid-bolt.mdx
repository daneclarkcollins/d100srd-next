# Acid Bolt

**Mana per Level:** 1  
**Casting Time:** 1 Standard Action  
**Range:** 60'  
**Target:** Single Target  
**Duration:** Instant  
**Avoidance:** Dodge/Parry  

A bolt of acid flies from the mage's hand toward the target. Does 1d4+1 damage per mana spent plus acts as Acid, causing 1 AV (Armor Value) damage for 1d4 rounds. 

Must use **Spot** skill to hit the target.

## Ranged Damage Spells - Aiming

Some spells, such as Acid Bolt or Elemental Arrow are treated as a ranged attack, though a mage uses **Spot** to aim the spell instead of the **Ranged Weapons** skill.

## Acid Effects

The acid continues to eat through armor and equipment for 1d4 rounds after the initial hit, reducing the target's Armor Value by 1 each round.
