# Invulnerable Sphere

*   Mana per Level: 1
*   Casting Time: 1 Standard Action
*   Range: 60’
*   Target: Single Target or Space
*   Duration: 1 minute
*   Avoidance: None
*   Special: Doubles duration and absorption
*   Type: Buff

The mage creates an invulnerable sphere that absorbs damage, protecting anyone inside. With a single mana worth of energy, the sphere is 5’ in diameter and absorbs 5 hit points worth of damage. For each mana level beyond the first, it can be expanded another 5’ or abosob another 5 damage. This does not block any objects coming out of the protected sphere, nor does it protects against non damaging spells.

Spellcraft failure: The spell fizzles, and mana is lost.
