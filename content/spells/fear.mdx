# Fear

*   Mana per Level: Varies
*   Casting Time: 1 Standard Action
*   Range: 60’
*   Target: Varies
*   Duration: 1 minute
*   Avoidance: Contested Roll Spirit vs. Spirit
*   Special: Doubles Duration
*   Type: Mind

You cause fear to creatures based on the mana spent. 

*   Mana 1: With a successful Spellcraft, a single target becomes Shaken.
*   Mana 3: With a successful Spellcraft, a single target becomes Scared.
*   Mana 5: With a successful Spellcraft, up to three creatures become Scared.
*   Mana 7: With a successful Spellcraft, up to three creatures become Panicked.

After the initial round, a Scared or Panicked creature may roll a Spirit check Vs the spellcaster’s Spellcraft skill. With a successful save, the target recovers and only becomes Shaken for the duration of the spell.

Spellcraft failure: The spell fizzles, and mana is lost.
