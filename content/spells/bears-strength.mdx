---
title: "Bear’s Strength"
order: 0
category: "spell"
description: "Bear’s Strength spell for SagaBorn D100"
---

*   Mana per Level: 2
*   Casting Time: 1 Standard Action
*   Range: Touch
*   Target: Single Target
*   Duration: 1 minute
*   Avoidance: None
*   Special: None
*   Type: Buff

The target becomes stronger, gaining magical strength.

*   2 Mana: +10 to STR
*   4 Mana: +20 to STR
*   6 Mana: +30 to STR

Spellcraft failure: The spell fizzles, and mana is lost.
