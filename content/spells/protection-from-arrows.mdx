# Protection from Arrows

*   Mana per Level: 1
*   Casting Time: 1 Standard Action
*   Range: Touch
*   Target: Single Target
*   Duration: 2 minutes per mana
*   Avoidance: Spirit
*   Special: Doubles duration
*   Type: Buff

Target becomes Difficult to hit with missile weapons. Each additional mana extends duration by 2 minutes or adds another protected target.

Spellcraft failure: The spell fizzles, and mana is lost.
