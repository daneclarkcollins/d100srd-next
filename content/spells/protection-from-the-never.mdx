---
title: "Protection from the Never"
order: 0
category: "spell"
description: "Protection from the Never spell for SagaBorn D100"
---

*   Mana per Level: 3
*   Casting Time: 1 Standard Action
*   Range: 30’
*   Target: Single object
*   Duration: 10 minutes per level
*   Avoidance: Acumen
*   Special: Doubles duration
*   Type: Buff

All in a 10’ radius of charmed object gain a +1 AV vs. Fey (Navirite) creatures, or protection from scrying through dreams.

Each mana level gives 10 minutes additional duration and sphere gains 5’.

Spellcraft failure: The spell fizzles, and mana is lost.
