category: "spell"
description: "Heal spell for SagaBorn D100"
---

*   Mana per Level: 1
*   Casting Time: 1 Standard Action
*   Range: Touch
*   Target: Single Target
*   Duration: Instant
*   Avoidance: Dodge
*   Special: Add 1d4 healing
*   Type: Healing

When laying your hand upon a living creature, you channel energy that cures the target.

1 Mana: heals 1d6 +3

Each additional mana adds 1d6+3

Spellcraft failure: The spell fizzles, and mana is lost.
