# Identify

*   Mana per Level: 1
*   Casting Time: 20 Minutes
*   Range: Touch
*   Target: Single Target
*   Duration: Instant
*   Avoidance: None
*   Special:
*   Type: Utility

The spell reveals all magical properties of a single item, including activation methods (if applicable) and any remaining charges. When used on an artifact, _Identify_ may not yield complete information but can offer hints about the artifact's abilities.

Spellcraft failure: The spell fizzles, and mana is lost.
