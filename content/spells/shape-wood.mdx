---
title: "Shape Wood"
order: 0
category: "spell"
description: "Shape Wood spell for SagaBorn D100"
---

*   Mana per Level: 1
*   Casting Time: 1 Standard Action
*   Range: 60’
*   Target: Single Target
*   Duration: Instant
*   Avoidance: Varies
*   Special:
*   Type: Utility

You shape wood into another form. Each mana level allows you to form a 5’x5’x5’ square of the material. If the material has already been crafted, shaped, or formed into an object, the spell becomes *difficult*. If the item is being used by a living being it becomes *very difficult* to reshape it.

Spellcraft failure: The spell fizzles, and mana is lost.
