# Sleep

*   Mana per Level: 1
*   Casting Time: 1 Standard Action
*   Range: 30’
*   Target: Single Target
*   Duration: Instant
*   Avoidance: Spirit
*   Special: Doubles Time; Critical: Triples Time
*   Type: Mind

The target of the spell falls asleep for 1 minute. Each mana level adds another minute.

*   Spellcraft failure: The spell fizzles, and mana is lost.
*   Spellcraft Fumble: The spell targets the caster.
