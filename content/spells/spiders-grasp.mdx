---
title: "Spider’s Grasp"
order: 0
category: "spell"
description: "Spider’s Grasp spell for SagaBorn D100"
---

*   Mana per Level: 1
*   Casting Time: 1 Standard Action
*   Range: Touch
*   Target: Single Target
*   Duration: 10 minutes
*   Avoidance: Dodge
*   Special: Double duration; Critical: Triple duration
*   Type: Utility

The target creature gains the ability to climb like a spider. When traveling up vertical or over ceilings, the creature can move at normal speeds. They must have at least three points of contract to stay attached. Slick, or difficult terrain on these surfaces slows the creature by half.

Spellcraft failure: The spell fizzles, and mana is lost.
