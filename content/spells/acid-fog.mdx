# Acid Fog

**Mana per Level:** 3  
**Casting Time:** 1 Standard Action  
**Range:** 60'  
**Target:** AoE 5' Radius Sphere per mana  
**Duration:** 1d4 rounds per mana  

Dense fog of corrosive acid fills the target area. Does 1d4 damage to creatures and reduces AV by 1 per round. Damages at casting and at the end of each turn spent in the fog.

## Area of Effect Spells

An Area of Effect (AoE) spell is one that damages more than one space.

## Targeting AoE Spells

When attempting to hit a specific space or target with an Area of Effect (AoE) spell, the caster must aim with the **Spot** skill. The mage must choose a target space and make a **Spot** skill check to hit it accurately.

### Fog Effects

- **Visibility:** Creatures inside the fog have severely limited visibility
- **Movement:** Movement through the fog is at half speed
- **Acid Damage:** Creatures take damage when they enter the fog and at the end of each turn they remain inside
