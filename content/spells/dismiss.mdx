title: "Dismiss"
order: 0
category: "spell"
description: "Dismiss spell for SagaBorn D100"
---

*   Mana per Level: 5
*   Casting Time: 1 Standard Action
*   Range: 60’
*   Target: Single Target per mana level
*   Duration: Instant
*   Avoidance: Contested Roll; Acumen vs. Acumen
*   Special: spell save becomes difficult for target; Critical: mage is automatically successful
*   Type: Debuff

This spell forces an extraplanar creature back to its proper plane if it fails a Heroic Action: Caster ACU vs. defender ACU. If the spell is successful, the creature is instantly whisked away to the Navirim.

Spellcraft failure: The spell fizzles, and mana is lost.
