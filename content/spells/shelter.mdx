# Shelter

*   Mana per Level: 1
*   Casting Time: 1 Standard Action
*   Range: 5’
*   Target: Single Space
*   Duration: 20 minutes
*   Avoidance: None
*   Special: Double the duration
*   Type: Utility

The mage creates a tent-like domed shelter that is 10’ wide and 5’ tall that protects the occupants from natural weather. Each additional mana gives an additional 5’ to each side and an additional 20 minutes.

The shelter can protect against temperatures from 0 degrees to 115 degrees, as well as snow to acidic rain. It will not protect from extreme events, like a magical ice storm or a rain of fiery lava.

Spellcraft failure: The spell fizzles, and mana is lost.
