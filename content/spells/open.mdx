---
title: "Open"
order: 0
category: "spell"
description: "Open spell for SagaBorn D100"
---

*   Mana per Level: Varies
*   Casting Time: 1 Standard Action
*   Range: Varies
*   Target: Single Target
*   Duration: Instant
*   Avoidance: None
*   Special: Doubles the range
*   Type: Utility

1 mana: You can open unlocked boxes, chests, and doors within 15’.

3 mana: You can open non-magical locked boxes, chests, and doors within 30’.

7 mana: You can open most magically locked boxes, chests, and doors within 50’. Anything locked by a magical ritual or artifact using spells of more than 7 mana will remain sealed.

Spellcraft failure: The spell fizzles, and mana is lost.
