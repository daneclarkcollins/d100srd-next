# Spirit Sap

*   Mana per Level: 3
*   Casting Time: 1 Standard Action
*   Range: 30’
*   Target: Single Target
*   Duration: Instant
*   Avoidance: Contested Roll Spirit vs. Spirit
*   Special:  Save _difficult_, Critical: Save _very difficult_
*   Type: Debuff

You drain spirit from the target into the ether. The target loses 1d3 Spirit Points from the target for every 3 mana spent. 

Spellcraft failure: The spell fizzles and doesn’t work.
