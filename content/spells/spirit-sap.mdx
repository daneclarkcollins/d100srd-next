---
title: "Spirit Sap"
order: 0
category: "spell"
description: "Spirit Sap spell for SagaBorn D100"
---

*   Mana per Level: 3
*   Casting Time: 1 Standard Action
*   Range: 30’
*   Target: Single Target
*   Duration: Instant
*   Avoidance: Contested Roll; Acumen vs. Acumen
*   Special: Save *difficult*, Critical: Save *very difficult*
*   Type: Debuff

You drain spirit from the target into the ether. The target loses 1d3 Spirit Points from the target for every 3 mana spent.

Spellcraft failure: The spell fizzles and doesn’t work.
