---
title: "Babble"
order: 0
category: "spell"
description: "Babble spell for SagaBorn D100"
---

*   Mana per Level: 1
*   Casting Time: 1 Standard Action
*   Range: 60’
*   Target: Single Target
*   Duration: Concentration
*   Avoidance: Acumen
*   Special: Save *difficult*, Critical: Save *very difficult*
*   Type: Mind

*Babble* causes the target only to be able to make incoherent noises and sounds. To themselves, they sound as if they are speaking normally, but others can not understand the strange noises they are making. The more mana imbued in the spell, the more serious the babbling becomes. The spell lasts as long as the spellcaster concentrates on it, though the target gets to attempt to save each round on their turn. Any mana level of *babble* causes all communication and spellcasting by the target to become *Difficult*.

1 Mana: The target's words become incoherent.

2 Mana: The target can not stop speaking, feeling compelled to talk about anything while no one understands them.

3 Mana: The target is frantically babbling, not even making sense to themselves, and all actions are considered *Difficult.*

Spellcraft failure: The spell fizzles, and mana is lost.
