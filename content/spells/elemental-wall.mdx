# Elemental Wall

*   Mana per Level: 1
*   Casting Time: 1 Standard Action
*   Range: 60’
*   Target: Single Target
*   Duration: 1 min per mana
*   Avoidance: None
*   Special: None
*   Type: Damage

You conjure a wall of elemental energy to block a path or create a protective ring around yourself. Choose an element you can control (fire, water, earth, air, ice, or electricity) for the wall’s energy.

*   **Mana 1**: A 10 ft. long, 10 ft. tall, and 1 ft. thick wall dealing 1d4+1 damage to anyone passing through it.
*   **Mana 3**: A 20 ft. long, 10 ft. tall, and 1 ft. thick wall dealing 3d4+3 damage to anyone passing through it.
*   **Mana 5**: A 40 ft. long, 10 ft. tall, and 1 ft. thick wall dealing 3d6+3 damage to anyone passing through. The wall can encircle the caster with a 5 ft. radius and can be positioned off-center, allowing space for other medium or small creatures.
*   **Mana 7**: A 50 ft. long, 10 ft. tall, and 1 ft. thick wall dealing 5d6+5 damage to anyone passing through. The wall can encircle the caster with a 10 ft. radius, with room for medium or small creatures if positioned off-center. Additionally, the caster may cause one side of the wall to emit energy, dealing 1d4+1 damage to anyone within 10 ft

Spellcraft failure: The spell fizzles, and mana is lost.
