---
title: "Vision"
order: 0
category: "spell"
description: "Vision spell for SagaBorn D100"
---

*   Mana per Level: 1
*   Casting Time: 1 Standard Action
*   Range: 50’
*   Target: Single Target
*   Duration: 1 minute
*   Avoidance: None
*   Special: Doubles duration and range; Critical: Triples duration and range
*   Type: Utility

The mage summons an invisible sphere within sight and transfers their sense to this sphere. This gives full vision and hearing, as if in that area, allowing that point of view to have any mobility they possess, while within the spell’s range.

Each additional mana level adds a minute to duration and 50’ to the range.

While using the Vision spell, the caster may not use any other spell or the Vision spell’s effects end.

Spellcraft failure: The spell fizzles, and mana is lost.
