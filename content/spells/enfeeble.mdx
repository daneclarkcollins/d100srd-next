# Enfeeble

*   Mana per Level: 1
*   Casting Time: 1 Standard Action
*   Range: 30’
*   Target: Single Target
*   Duration: Instant
*   Avoidance: Stamina
*   Special: Add an extra 5%; Critical Add 10%
*   Type: Debuff

The target of the spell becomes weaker and its next skill check is -5%. Each mana level adds another negative 5%.

*   Spellcraft failure: The spell fizzles, and mana is lost.
*   Spellcraft Fumble: The spell targets the caster.
