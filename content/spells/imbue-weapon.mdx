# Imbue Weapon

*   Mana per Level: Varies
*   Casting Time: 1 Standard Action
*   Range: Touch
*   Target: Single weapon
*   Duration: 2 minutes
*   Avoidance: None
*   Special: Doubles duration
*   Type: Buff

The target's weapon becomes more lethal.

*   1 Mana: The weapon gains +5% to hit and +1 damage.
*   3 Mana: The weapon gains +10% to hit and +2 damage.
*   5 Mana: The weapon gains +15% to hit and +3 damage.

Spellcraft failure: The spell fizzles, and mana is lost.
