# Aid

**Mana per Level:** 1  
**Casting Time:** 1 Standard Action  
**Range:** Touch  
**Duration:** 1 minute  

Target gains +10% per mana spent on a chosen skill until that skill is used.

## Skill Enhancement

The Aid spell provides a temporary bonus to any single skill of the caster's choice. The bonus remains until the enhanced skill is attempted, whether the attempt succeeds or fails.

### Stacking

Multiple Aid spells do not stack on the same skill. If a new Aid spell is cast on the same skill, it replaces the previous bonus.

### Examples

- **Aid (2 mana) on Stealth:** +20% to the next Stealth check
- **Aid (1 mana) on Persuasion:** +10% to the next Persuasion check
- **Aid (3 mana) on Climb:** +30% to the next Climb check
