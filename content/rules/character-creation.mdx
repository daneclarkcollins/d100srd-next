# Character Creation

Welcome to SagaBorn character creation! Follow these steps to create your hero.

## Step 1: Choose Your Species

The first step is selecting your character's species:
- **Terian**: Native species of Atheles
- **Fey**: Otherworldly beings
- **Elven**: Ancient and mysterious

## Step 2: Roll or Build Characteristics

You have two options:
- **Random Roll**: Roll 3d6 for each characteristic
- **Point Buy**: Start with 10 in each stat and spend 24 points
