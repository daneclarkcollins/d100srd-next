# Apparition

[![](http://localhost:8200/wp-content/uploads/2025/08/apparition-705x1024.jpg)](http://localhost:8200/wp-content/uploads/2025/08/apparition.jpg)

Art by Michael Bielaczyc

_A wisp of fluttering light is all that can be seen on the material plane, but in the In-Between, the apparition retains its form from its birth . . . whatever that may be._  

An apparition has been driven completely insane by its time trapped in the In-Between. I first ran into one in the farms outside of Fort Deadrick. Farmers had been found drained of life, lifeless husks littered haphazardly throughout their village during one of their local festivals. On my approach, I was attacked by a spectral creature—nothing more than a shimmering bit of mist in the fading light of day. My normal blades did no good, but a silver dagger caused it harm. It escaped our first encounter, but I tracked it to an ancient stone ring in the nearby woods. There, we battled until it fell, its deathly light stricken from our world.

—Etahn

**CV**: 5  
**Class**: Monster  
**Type**: Navirite (Fey)  
**Speed**: Fly 60  
**HP**: 45**Damage Modifier**: -  
**Armor Value**: 5**Attack**: **_Material Plane:_** Touch 75% 1d6 negative energy (magical damage) and Spirit Point Drain; **_In-Between_**_:_ Claws (x2) 70% 1d8+1d4 Spirit Point Drain plus Health Drain  
**Skills**: Acrobatics +30%, Awareness +100% (includes +50% for Heightened Senses), Dodge 60%,  Knowledge +20%, Spellcraft +20%, Survival +20%  
**Special**: Constitution Drain, Resilient Aura, Sense the Living, Heightened Senses, Dual Plane Damage Modes  
**Weaknesses**: Silver and Amarnium weapons can damage as if the Apparition were corporeal.  
**Horror**: First Encounter 3/2d6+1  
**Treasure**: None  

**Spirit Point Drain**When the apparition hits with a **touch attack**, the target must succeed on a **Stamina check.** If it is successful, they suffer -1 Spirit Points, if unsuccessful, they suffer -1d6+1 Spirit Points

**In-Between**If a creature is in the **In-Between**, the apparition uses its claws (x2) to attack instead of its touch. Each successful claw hit grants the apparition Health Drain against the target, healing it **3 HP**.

**Sense the Living**Apparitions are drawn to the presence of the living. In the In-Between, they can attack normally.

**Heightened Senses**Apparitions have an otherworldly perception. They gain **+50% to Awareness** checks.**Resilient Aura**Though lacking a physical form, the apparition’s supernatural aura grants it **45 HP**.
